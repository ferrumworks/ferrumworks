A two-week phase modpack made by a group of friends.

Designed to deliver a stable, consistent vanilla+ experience with carefully selected tech mods. Best enjoyed during relaxed evening game sessions.

### Performance & Visuals

*   Built on the **Fabulously Optimized** performance stack
*   **Sodium**, **Lithium**, **ModernFix**, **FerriteCore**, **ImmediatelyFast**
*   **Iris** shaders support
*   Better rendering, culling, and overall performance

### Engineering & Storage

*   **Create** + **Create Crafts & Additions** + **Copycats+**
*   **Applied Energistics 2**
*   **Tom's Simple Storage**

### World & Exploration

*   **Terralith**
*   **Tectonic**
*   **Traveler's Backpack**
*   **Artifacts**

### Building & Utilities

*   **WorldEdit**
*   **Carry On**
*   **Vein Mining**

### Quality of Life

*   **EMI** + AE2, Enchanting & Loot integrations
*   **Jade** + Jade Addons
*   **Xaero's Minimap** & **World Map**
*   **Mouse Tweaks**
*   Ping Wheel, Zoomify, Legendary Tooltips, Item Borders, and many other small improvements.
